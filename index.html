Robin Ren

PhD Student at Eyethink Lab, University College London

'Hello Viewer' (If you know James May you know what this is). I'm a PhD student in Experimental Psychology. My main research looks at audience engagement, and we use heart rate synchrony to quantify this multidimensional concept. More grounded, I'm interested in how do social contexts influence audience engagament. Imagine watching a theatre play in the actual theatre, and its livestreamed version at home alone. Almost everyone (within 95% confidence interval/ or credible interval if you are a bayesian) would say the actual theatre experience is better. And we wanted to know why? Our answer lies in social context, that I believe, from personal experience and previous research, the emotions, behvaiours, responses from the audience around us endow the collective experience a certain uniqueness that manifested in audience engagement. Outside of research, I am a 'lifelong' supporter of Man United, huge fan of cinema and theatres, I just enjoy a well-delivered narrative! Other than that, I take some pretty pictures of things I like this the world, and I am (was?) a content creator/vlogger. Those probably will appear in the self/ego section, but no promises!
